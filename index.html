Who is Mirrix made for?

Product Managers

Ensure every product is matched with the right visuals, documents, and metadata—automatically. Mirrix uses AI to streamline asset linking and maintain product accuracy across channels.

Marketing Teams

Quickly find, verify, and download brand-compliant assets. Mirrix makes campaign execution faster and safer by managing rights, cutouts, and expiration dates in one place.

STEP solution owners

Mirrix runs natively on your existing STEP model—no duplication, no external syncs, no governance gaps. It brings DAM capabilities into STEP with full control and integration.

Image Editors

Launch assets directly into Photoshop, Illustrator, or Canva—and send them back into STEP with version control and metadata preserved. No more renaming or file juggling.

Sales teams

Always have access to the latest approved images and documents. Mirrix makes it easy to retrieve and reuse assets for pitches, portals, and customer presentations—without waiting on others.

Customer Support

Respond faster and more visually. Mirrix helps you find the right product image, manual, or spec sheet in seconds to assist customers accurately and efficiently.

Suppliers

Upload, tag, and enrich your own images and documents in a shared environment that respects your client's STEP structure and workflows—without needing STEP access.

Distributors/Customers

Access high-quality, approved digital assets with the right language, resolution, and rights for your market. Mirrix ensures consistency across regions and platforms.

External agencies

Collaborate directly in workflows that streamline approval, tagging, and delivery. Mirrix gives your agency partners a clean, controlled interface that speaks STEP—without exposing backend complexity.
